Simon Says Game

How To Play

When pressing the "start game" button, the computer will highlight the same amount of random tiles as the level starting at level 1.

Once the sequence is complete, you have to repeat it exactly. If you do not then you shall lose one of your 3 lives.

Upon losing all 3 lives the game is over and you are beaten.

lives do not recover until page is refreshed or a new game is started.
